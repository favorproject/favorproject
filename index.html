Citation Excellence: NALT Format

NALT citations are the cornerstone of professional medical and healthcare research. Our writers ensure every reference is formatted with precision and scholarly integrity.

‚úì NALT Citation Example:
Glenn Cohen and Michelle Mello, 'HIPAA and Protecting Health Information in the 21st Century' Journal of the American Medical Association (2018) 320(3) 231, 232.

This exemplifies our approach: author names, article title in single quotes, journal title in plain text, publication year in parentheses, volume, issue, and page numbers‚Äîall formatted to exacting academic standards.

Citation Mastery: OSCOLA Format

OSCOLA is the gold standard for legal scholarship. We expertly format every legal citation, footnote, and reference to meet OSCOLA requirements with absolute precision.

‚úì OSCOLA Citation Example:
Anuradha G and others, 'Child marriage in India: A study of its socioeconomic impact and policy perspective' (2018) 8(2) Asian Journal of Research in Social Sciences and Humanities 18, 34.

Notice the precision: multiple authors cited as 'and others', article title in single quotes, journal name fully italicized, year in parentheses, volume and issue numbers, and specific page references. This is the FavorProject standard.
